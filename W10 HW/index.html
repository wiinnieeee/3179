WORLD DISASTER RISK

From 2010 to 2021

Disasters are sudden events that always cause widespread destruction, major damage or loss of life, either done by forces of nature or human beings. Disasters happen frequently across the globe, from small sovereigns to major countries. In WorldRiskReport, several indicators are used to determine the disaster risks for countries and regions worldwide. The higher the World Risk Index (WRI) of the country, the higher its national disaster risks.
